Who We Are

The Dancing Fools have been teaching social dance lessons in Boston for over 20 years. We specialize in west coast swing because it can be danced just about anywhere and uses today's current music- everything from hip-hop, to popular music, to R&B, to country western and more!

We will teach you in a fun, relaxed atmosphere. No partner necessary; bring friends or meet new people! Our ability to incorporate good technique, musicality, and the sheer delight of being playful and having fun with each "fool" we dance with is what makes us The Dancing Fools!

West Coast Swing

West coast swing is a flexible, slow swing dance that is danced to a wide variety of music: rhythm and blues, 60s, latin, 70s funk, 80s pop, 90s boy bands, and hip hop. It is a more sultry and sexier style of swing dancing than Lindy Hop. West coast swing is the official state dance of California. It has been gaining national attention recently, with west coast swing dancers appearing on a variety of TV shows.
